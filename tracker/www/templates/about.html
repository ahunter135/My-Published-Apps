How To
On the directory page, press the + button to add and rate a food item. You can then use the magnifying glass to search your whole directory. Press the menu button at the top to change to the Journal page. You can then select a date to write a journal entry and choose how many bowel movements you had that day.
Press and hold on a food item to delete it or to go to a details page which allows you to add ingredients to each food item.
If you upgrade to premium using the in-app purchase, you will have the ability to make an account and backup your data. So when you change devices, you can redownload the app and get your data back.
